WhoAmI?

2020 was a hell of a year for the majority of people. Covid-19 hit and suddenly almost every person I knew was stuck inside with nothing to do and no one to see. This happened to me slap bang in the middle of my economics degree, and suddenly I had 2 months of lockdown ahead of me (or at least I thought it would be 2 months).

Unlike most people, I did not see this as an opportunity to binge drink for sixty days (I was a student, I did not need a sixty-day lockdown as an excuse to do that). I instead saw this as 60 straight days where suddenly I had all the time in the world to try and sort my life out.I changed a lot during that period, but the most transient change (and most relevant to this blog) is I became enamoured with technology.

I’ve learned a lot since that time and I am continuing to learn to this day, and I created this blog to share those snippets of information. My interests mainly focus on privacy, security, Python3, and cryptocurrency; but I imagine at some point ill do a ranty blog about something that has nothing to do with these.
